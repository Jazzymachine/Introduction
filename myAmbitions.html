My ambition

The image below is meant to summarize the most important aspects of my ambition:

 

My ambition lies in becoming an expert in what I do, NOT in climbing the company ladder

I want to love what I do. I want a job that motivates me to get out of bed every morning. I want to feel like I'm spending my workhours usefully.

I find the idea of having the ability to create things just by writing code, very appealing. That's why I decided to use the image of Bob the Builder to explain my ambition. Because he can build things with his own hands and all the skills he tought himself along the way.
